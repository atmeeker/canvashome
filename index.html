Welcome to the CPD Canvas Course!  

The W&L Office of Career and Professional Development (CPD) uses Canvas as a platform for sharing information and updates about career-related opportunities and resources with current students. This content is located on Canvas, so the information is easily accessible to you in the same place you go for your academic classes. The CPD Canvas Course is for informational purposes only; no academic assignments or grades are associated with this "course."

Getting Started

Your first step is to complete the "Get Started" module. This will introduce you to the CPD Canvas Course and guide you through the process of selecting groups. To access CPD's Career Resources and receive industry-specific information, you must complete this first step.
